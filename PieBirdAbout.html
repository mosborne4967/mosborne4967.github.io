Our Mission

When we tell our customers "Good Morning" we don't just say it, we create it! Here at Pie Bird Cafe we believe that the best start to any happy productive day is to have a wholesome and satisfying breakfast. Thus, our mission statement and promise that "Good Mornings, Create Your Good Days."

Founders

Tiffany Osborne graduated with an associates in Culinary Arts while interning at Sundance Mountain Resort. Afterwards she moved to St. George for her bachelors in Hospitality Management. While there, she saw the opportunity and need for breakfast services and partnered with her sister.

Mary Osborne graduated with an associates in Graphic Design and a bachelors in Business Management. After interning with Deseret News in both their publication and web departments she joined in her sisters' endeavor to create the Pie Bird Cafe.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide a balanced breakfast that is satisfying, wholesome, and a relaxing experience to our customers.

  • Satisfying: Thanks to the most important meal, when you eat our nutrient dense breakfasts you'll feel satisfied and filled with more energy throughout the day!
  • Wholesome: We believe in cooking our food correctly to respect the produce and preserve the rich natural flavors that heighten your senses. We avoid excess fats, sugars, and preservatives which dull the palate and leave you ever still hungry afterwards.
  • Relaxing: whether you're a busybody or a chill-bug; our cafe will provide you with a stress-free environment
